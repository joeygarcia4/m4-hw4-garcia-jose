About Parcels

Parcels is an Australian electropop five-piece formed in Byron Bay, Australia, in 2014. Today they are based in Berlin, Germany. The band's line-up is composed of keyboardist Louie Swain, keyboardist/guitarist Patrick Hetherington, bassist Noah Hill, drummer Anatole "Toto" Serret, and guitarist Jules Crommelin.

Their music features groovy basslines, intricate rhythms, and modern electronic influences, making them a distinctive act in contemporary pop and indie music.
